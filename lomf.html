The people I admire

"My Girlfriend"

My girlfriend is one of the person I admire. When I first met her, I can say that the change in my life is so big. She always gives me courage for every challenge of life. She opened my heart more to the Lord. Whenever I feel weak, he is always there to strengthen and build me up. I am very proud of my girlfriend, because she has not only been a good daughter to her mother; she has also been good to my parents. My girlfriend is like falling from the sky, and I was given a woman like her.

My parents

My parents are one of my most admired people in life. They were my strength whenever I was weak when I was young; they gave me what I needed as a child; they developed me, so I became a person that I want to be. Without them, Harwin wouldn't be here today. I love my parents very much, and my admiration and love for them will never change.
