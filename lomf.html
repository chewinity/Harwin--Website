The people I admire

"My Girlfriend"

My girlfriend is one of the persons I admire. My life changed a lot the time I met her. She helped me become closer to the Lord. Whenever I feel weak, she is always there to strengthen and build me up. I am very proud that she's my girlfriend, because she is not only a good daughter to her mother, but also to my parents. She is like a blessing from above, because I am grateful that I have a woman like her.

My parents

My parents are one of my most admired people in life. They were my strength whenever I was weak, When I was young, they provides me my needs. They developed me, to became the person that I want to be. Without them, Harwin wouldn't be here today. I love my parents very much, and my admiration and love for them will never change.
